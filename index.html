Hi, I'm Timo – a composer based in Mosbach, Germany, specializing in production music for advertising, media and sync licensing.

My love for music started at age 11 when I picked up the French horn and played in various school orchestras. At 16, I taught myself bass guitar and started diving into beat production and larger, more dynamic compositions.

Today I work from my home studio creating everything from cinematic orchestral tracks to high-energy sport and action music – always with a personal, creative touch.

My music has been placed in international broadcasts, including as background music during the EHF Handball European Championship on ZDF. I work with agencies, brands and media productions to create tailor-made music that fits their projects perfectly – licensed, flexible and always on time.

Feel free to reach out – I'd love to hear about your project.
